Brad's Larping

One of Brad's favorite hobbies is LARPing. He enjoys getting to dress up and show off what he can do with a bow and a dagger when he gets the chance. You can see here that he likes to use his bow and takes a clean shot.

You can also see here that he runs around quite a bit and will use both his dagger and bow at the same time. Able to hold both in one hand well shooting and able to then shift and use the bow with his free hand.

The Character

Brad's LARP character is known as Baron Xarzale Lord of the Deep, Marquee of Belfalas. He is a commander of the fort The Deep in the Lore of Eryendor and also has been given the status of Marquee over the city of Belfalas after the banker rebellion failed.

Here you can see him dressed in the colors of the Fighting Cockatris wearing the Orange, blue, and Green of their small group. This is the outfit is one that he enjoys wearing out to the Renfair when it is in Michigan. The lovely headpice comes from his wifes collection.
